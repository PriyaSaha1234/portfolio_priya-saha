Word Anagram

A word puzzle game that challenges players to solve anagrams, improve vocabulary, and test their pattern-recognition skills.

Overview


World Anagram is an interactive word game designed to make learning and problem-solving engaging through challenging anagram-based puzzles. The project focused on delivering a clean, responsive, and enjoyable user experience while maintaining smooth gameplay across devices. The goal was to create a lightweight yet addictive experience that encourages users to think critically, expand vocabulary, and keep returning to improve their scores.
