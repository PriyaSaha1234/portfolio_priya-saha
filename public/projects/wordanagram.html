Word Anagram

A word puzzle game that challenges players to solve anagrams, improve vocabulary, and test their pattern-recognition skills.

Overview


Word Anagram is a word puzzle game that challenges players to unscramble words across multiple difficulty levels while improving vocabulary and problem-solving skills.

The application utilizes DFS and A* search algorithms for solution generation and comparison, complemented by real-time feedback, scoring mechanics, and interactive gameplay features.
